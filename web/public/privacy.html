What We Collect

When you use Samva, we collect: your WhatsApp phone number, name, messages you send to Sam (in self-chat only), email configuration if you connect it, and any data you explicitly ask Sam to remember.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to power Sam — your personal assistant. We use it to: respond to your messages, store your preferences and memories, send you morning briefs and reminders, read your emails (only if you connected them), and improve Sam's understanding of your needs through Soul Evolution.

Data Isolation

Each user's data is completely isolated. No user can see another user's messages, contacts, memories, or business information. Your data is stored in a secured PostgreSQL database on Railway's infrastructure.

Third-Party Services

We use: Google Gemini and OpenAI for AI processing, OpenRouter for LLM routing, Razorpay for payments, Twilio for voice calls, and Railway for hosting. Your messages are processed by these AI services to generate responses but are not stored by them beyond the request.

Email Security

If you connect your email, your app password is encrypted using AES-256 (Fernet) encryption before storage. We never store your regular Gmail password — only the app-specific password you generate.

Voice Notes

Voice notes you send are transcribed to text for processing and are not stored permanently as audio files. The transcription is stored as part of your conversation history.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of all your data by emailing hello@samva.in. We will delete your account, messages, memories, contacts, and all associated data within 7 business days.

No Selling of Data

We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data in any way. Your data is used solely to provide and improve Sam's service to you.
