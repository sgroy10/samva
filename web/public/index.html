Questions

Everything you need to know.

How does Sam get on my WhatsApp?+

After you pay, you get a QR code. Open WhatsApp → Settings → Linked Devices → Link a Device → scan the QR. Sam connects as a linked device — like WhatsApp Web. Your number stays yours. Sam just reads and sends messages through it.

What happens after I scan the QR?+

Sam sends you a message on WhatsApp and starts a short conversation to learn about you — what you do, your prices, your rules, your language. This takes about 5 minutes. After that, Sam is fully set up and starts helping you immediately. You'll get your first morning brief the next day.

What can Sam actually do?+

Answer messages — Sam responds to people who message your WhatsApp, in their language, using your business knowledge.

Read & send email — "Check my mail" gets you a summary. Ramble in Hindi, Sam drafts a polished English email.

Scan business cards — Photo any card, Sam saves the contact forever. Ask "Vikram ka number?" anytime.

Meeting notes — Voice note after any meeting. Sam structures everything, saves contacts, sets reminders, emails you a summary.

Reminders — One-time or recurring. Birthdays, payments, follow-ups. Sam never forgets.

Gold brief — For jewellers: 24K, 22K, 18K rates every morning with buy/hold view.

Stock alerts — "Watch Reliance, alert above ₹1450." Sam tracks and notifies.

Web search — Current info: weather, news, prices, anything.

Memory — "My COD charge is ₹50." Sam remembers permanently.

What languages does Sam speak?+

Sam automatically detects and matches your language. Hindi, English, Gujarati, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, Punjabi, Hinglish, and more. You can even switch languages mid-conversation — Sam keeps up.

Can Sam answer my customers?+

Yes. When someone messages your WhatsApp, Sam answers using the knowledge you gave during onboarding — your prices, policies, delivery info, and rules. Sam will escalate to you for anything it's not sure about. You control what Sam handles vs what gets forwarded to you.

Is my data safe?+

Yes. Each user's data is completely isolated — no one can see your conversations, contacts, or business information. Email passwords are encrypted. Voice notes are processed and not stored permanently. We never share or sell your data.

How do I connect my email?+

Just message Sam: "connect email your@gmail.com your_app_password". For Gmail, you need an App Password (not your regular password) — Sam will guide you through creating one. Works with Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, Zoho, GoDaddy, and any IMAP email.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. No commitment, no lock-in. Your subscription is monthly. Cancel anytime and Sam pauses at the end of your billing cycle. Your data stays safe — reactivate anytime and Sam picks up right where it left off.

What if Sam doesn't know the answer?+

Sam is honest. If Sam doesn't know something, it says so — "Main check karke batati hoon" — and escalates to you. Sam never guesses prices, policies, or makes commitments without your approval. The more you use Sam, the more it learns.
